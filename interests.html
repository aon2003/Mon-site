My Interests

My name is Ahmed, and since I was a child, I have always been a bit different from other kids. I had no interest in typical childhood games or video games. Instead, I was more drawn to adults discussing important topics. I always paid close attention to learn from them, develop my own opinions, and become an active citizen with my own voice in my country. That's why I became passionate about politics and economics. This passion was further reinforced by the country where I lived previously.
