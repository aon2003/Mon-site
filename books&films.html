My favourite books & films

I love reading a lot, but I prefer listening even more. I have been fascinated by some historical books that tell stories from the past. I find this truly wonderful as it allows us to explore the past that forms the foundation of the present. Being of Egyptian origin and opposed to the military regime that has ruled Egypt since 1952, I became interested in reading a book written by the leader of the first military coup in modern Egyptian history, "I was the President of Egypt." This book was authored by the first President of Egypt under the republican system. He was overthrown by one of his aides in the previous coup. The book is available in both French and English.
